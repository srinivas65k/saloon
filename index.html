Why Online Beauty & Spa?

Getting a Hair Cut from your neighborhood barber is time-taking. Most Barbershops have long wait times or have an unreliable system to book & Schedule appointments. Luckily those problems can be solved.
Now, Users can Schedule, Cancel, Book & Pay their Barber Online all from One Mobile App.
Best of all, It is FREE to use!

24/7 Scheduling

Users can easily book appointments at their convenience 24/7. Thanks to our online & always-on Scheduling system!

Simple & Quick Payment

Users can pay through the app & barbers instantly cash out at any time, making easier.

No Show Protection

Cancellations & no-drama, so we've built a reliable system to ensure Barbers are paid for their time.

Busy Time Pricing

Charging higher rates on your busier days/times & holidays with our Busy time pricing feature.

Completely Flexible

Pricing & Flexible Schedule. Set your own pricing, breaks, days-off, peak times & Cancellation policy. *Based on Pack Chosen.

Yearly One-time Subscriptions

Free to download for Barbers & Users.

Licensed Barbers/Stylists Only!

Our site is designed specifically for licensed Barbers and Hair Stylists that don't have enough time or resources to manage a complicated appointment scheduling software & much more.
Furthermore, we focus our site on BARBER APPOINTMENTS ONLY. Not dentists, nail salons, massage parlors, etc.
We’re proud to say that we’re the only app focused on helping licensed Barbers to manage & grow their businesses with ease.
